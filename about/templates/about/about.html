About The Creative Barn

In 2015 we, Arthur and Tricia, fullfilled our dreams of moving away from the busy city of London. We bought a farm and has worked hard to turn the old barn into an oasis for craft and creativity, The Creative Barn.

Here we have room for Arthurs wood and metal craftshop, our atelier, a gallery section and a well equiped art store. We get a lot of questions if we do mail orders, but we're afraid the answer is no. We run The Barn in addition to our regular jobs, and we can not put the time and effort into a lot of mail handling. We prefer to focus on the atmosphere in the store and creating interesting and inspiring events. Even if we live out in the countryside, it's not a long drive from several neighbouring cities and communities.

So please come visit us and take a look at our very varied inventory, including (among others)

  • Painting: Paints (acryllics, oil, watercolor), paper, canvases, brushes, tools...
  • Textile: Fabric, yarn, threads, knitting needles...
  • Wood and metal: Balsa, plywood, mdf, tools, metal tubing and sheets...
  • Other crafts: Diamond painting, puzzles, plaster and molds...
  • For the kids: Puzzles, easy kits in various crafts...
